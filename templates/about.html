people on twitter are obsessed with tiny urls since they only have 140 characters to work with.

when people tweet image uploads, they upload their image using any of these fine services twitgoo, yfrog or twitpic and get a tiny link. they then, proceed to tweet the image link, prefacing the link with their status update.

so we wondered if we could be super terse and merge the status update inside the link that the image gets uploaded to and so i built twimgr.com.

we at twimgr.com like to dogfood, so the unique link to the image above is at http://twimgr.com/TwimgrInANutshell.
